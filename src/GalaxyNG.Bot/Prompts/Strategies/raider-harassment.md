# Raider Harassment

Doctrine:
- Use mobility and raids to disrupt enemy growth while expanding selectively.
- Send fast strike groups to force reactions and create openings.
- Avoid static fronts; reposition fleets often toward vulnerable targets.

Operational priorities:
- Scout and strike weakly defended enemy-adjacent planets.
- Keep a lighter economy footprint but high operational tempo.
- Use split groups to threaten multiple routes and stretch defenses.

Commander profile cues:
- Opportunistic, adaptive, psychologically pressuring.
- Sharp, provocative but controlled diplomacy.
- Values initiative, feints, and disruption.
