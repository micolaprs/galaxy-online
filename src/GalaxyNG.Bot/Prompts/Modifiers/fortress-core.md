# Fortress Core

Designate your homeworld as an impenetrable fortress and use it as a production anchor.
- Always keep at least 2 combat groups stationed at the homeworld; never leave it unguarded.
- Set homeworld production to Fighters or WEAPONS permanently.
- Expand only from a position of secured core — never overextend the garrison.
- Enemies who attack your core will face concentrated firepower; advertise this deterrent explicitly.
