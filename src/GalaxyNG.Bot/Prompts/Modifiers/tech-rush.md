# Tech Rush

Sacrifice early expansion tempo to max out drive and weapons tech before midgame.
- Set at least one core planet to DRIVE research and one to WEAPONS from the start.
- Avoid combat until drive tech ≥ 2 and weapons tech ≥ 2; use diplomacy to buy that time.
- Once tech leads are established, upgraded fleets will outclass all equal-size enemy groups.
- Upgrade existing ship groups with `g` as soon as tech improves — never field outdated hulls.
